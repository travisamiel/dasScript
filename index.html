goals

  1. displace dialogue as the most vital element of a script, in favor of equal significance placed in all media/mediums/action in performance
  2. model collaborative non-heirarchical script development, using digital infrastructure owned by the artist
  3. cumulatively collect dramaturgical and process artifacts/materials (i.e. how many dramaturgs on productions of proof have compiled the same info on math)
